Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Who can resist these warm gooey chocolate chip cookies? It's a classic for a reason! whether you serve them with milk, ice cream or just fresh out of the oven you'll be sure to come back for seconds (and maybe even thirds)

Ingredients

  • 1 cup salted butter
  • 1 cupe white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat ofen to 375 degrees F. Line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside.
  2. In a seperate bowl mid flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. Set aside
  3. Cream together butter and sugards until combined.
  4. Beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy.
  5. Mix in dry igredients until combined.
  6. Add 12 oz package of chocolate chips and mix well.
  7. Roll 2-3 tbsp of dough at a time into balls and place them evenly spaced on your prepared cookie sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven for approximately 8010 minutes. Take them out when they are just BARELY starting to turn brown.
  9. Let them sit on the pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack
